      NLP Online Courses

        Courses on Offer

        NLP Online courses are interactive one-on-one and group sessions with Ashok Subramanian over web based video-conferencing. Currently there are 3 types of courses on offer: Personal Effectiveness Workshop (PEW), NLP Foundation Workshop (FW) and Personal Excellence Coaching (telephonic).

        Format & Requirements

        Each PEW and FW course consists of six 2-hr interactive sessions distributed over a period of 3 weeks and includes assignments and a certificate (you'll need a decent internet connection and a webcam).Telephonic coaching is a counselling session over phone and can be purchased/scheduled when required.
